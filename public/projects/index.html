Projects

I conduct projects in the area of social science. I have presented my work at the Worcester Regional Science and Engineering Fair, the Massachusetts State Science and Engineering Fair, the International Science and Engineering Fair, the National Junior Science and Humanities Symposium, and the IEEE Integrated STEM Education Conference. I have also been selected as a Broadcom MASTERS semifinalist.

    Predicting Opioid Use Disorder (OUD) Using Machine Learning - March 2019
    This project uses a random forest classifier to predict adults at risk for developing opioid use disorder.
    Predicting and Understanding Suicidal Thoughts and Behaviors - March 2019
    This project uses a decision tree classifier to predict individuals at risk for suicidal ideation.
    The Demographic Divide in the Advanced Placement Curriculum - March 2018
    This project evaluates how race, gender, and age affect participation and performance in the AP curriculum
    The Pursuit of Happiness - March 2017
    This project analzes and compares, on an average, what life events make people particularly happy. It also compares the similarity of life events using the Hellinger Distance.
